mewshifts

MEW Shifts is a flexible shift management app designed for employees working rotating, night, day, or custom schedules across various industries.

Key Features

  • Custom shift patterns and rotations
  • Attendance tracking (Attend / Leave)
  • Offline-first design
  • Optional cloud backup and restore
  • Multi-language support

Account & Cloud Backup

Sign-in is optional and required only for cloud backup and restore features. Users may delete their account at any time from within the app.
